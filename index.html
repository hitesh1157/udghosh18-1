About

UDGHOSH

IIT KANPUR has been one of the leading centers of education and research since its inception , providing some of the brightest minds of the country.

Udghosh has always been a classic example of the fact that it's the endeavor that counts . Our efforts have always aimed to bring about a rise in comptetion level among the youths. empowering the masses with a torch of social awareness , keeping the frandeur of entertainment and professional shows alive. Udghosh brings to the masses a plethoral of events , comprising all major sports , workshops and informal events.

Campus Ambassador

Who are Campus Ambassador and what they do?

Udghosh, IIT Kanpur is India’s Largest College Sports festival with a total footfall of more than 60 thousand, a reach of more than 200 colleges in India and over 2500 participants. In the past 21 years, Udghosh, IIT Kanpur has worked to promote Sportsmanship spirit, leadership skills, and unification among the youth. A college ambassador is instrumental in igniting this spirit among the young minds of their college.
